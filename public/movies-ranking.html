The Best Movies According to Agastya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Story of 3 friends & their engineering life shown in movie. Love what you do and do what you love is the main theme of the movie.

Persuit of Hapiness

Movie displayed the struggle of a family man to getting a dream job and poverty doesnot matters if you have the dedication to achieve the goal shown in movie

Kantara

Story of coastal village people and belief on the demi god beautifully represented in this movie. Comedy, action, love and thriller scenes are the highlights of the movie.
